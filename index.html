About Alpine Secondary School

Alpine is a secondary school is located in Shreepur-11, Birgunj. It was established in 1997. The school is known for its quality education and extracurricular activities. The school offers education from grade Nursery to grade 12, following the national curriculum. It has a team of dedicated teachers and staff who work towards the holistic development of students. The school also organizes various events and competitions to encourage students to showcase their talents. Overall, Alpine secondary school is a reputable institution in the Birgunj area.

Affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and recognized by the Ministry of Education, Alpine Secondary School has continuously produced outstanding results in SEE and +2 programs.

Academic Programs

The school offers a comprehensive academic structure designed to meet national and global standards:

  • Early Childhood Development (Nursery-UKG)
  • Primary Level (Grade 1–5)
  • Lower Secondary Level (Grade 6–8)
  • Secondary Level (Grade 9–10 – SEE)
  • Higher Secondary (+2 Science & Management)

School Facilities

  • Well-equipped Science and Computer Laboratories
  • Extensive Library with academic resources
  • Smart classrooms with modern teaching tools

  • Safe transportation services
  • Clean cafeteria and hygienic environment

Co-Curricular & Extra-Curricular Activities

Alpine Secondary School strongly believes in holistic education. Students are encouraged to participate in various activities that enhance leadership, creativity, and confidence.

  • Sports competitions and physical training
  • Music, dance, and cultural programs
  • Debates, quizzes, and speech competitions
  • Educational tours and workshops
  • Annual functions and social awareness programs
